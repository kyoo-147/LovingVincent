"In the life of the painter, death may perhaps not be the most difficult thing. For myself, i declare i don't know anything about it but the side of the stars always makes me dream. Why i say to myself ship the spots of the light in the firrmament be inaccessible to us. Maybe we can take death to go to a star and to die peacefully of old age would be to go there on the foot. For the moment, i'm going to go to bed because it's late and i wish you good night and good luck with a handshake. You're Loving Vincent!"
